** App Store description **

Tagged brings the power of the TimeTagger web app to your iPhone — giving you a fast, privacy‑friendly way to track your time anywhere. Built around TimeTagger’s interactive timeline and flexible tag-based workflow, the companion app keeps your records synced, organized, and ready for reporting.
Track your work, study sessions, client projects, or daily routines with a single tap. Use tags instead of rigid projects to stay flexible.
Key Features
• Tag-based workflow for flexible categorization instead of fixed projects 
• Offline tracking with automatic sync across devices 
• Privacy-first: no cookies, no tracking, no third‑party services 
• Open-source, developer-friendly
Whether you’re a freelancer, developer, creator, or simply want to understand where your time goes, Tagged helps you stay organized, productive, and in control — all from your iPhone.
