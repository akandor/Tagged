## Description (limit 4000)

**Tagged brings the power of TimeTagger to your iPhone — a fast, private, and genuinely pleasant way to track where your time goes.**

Start a session, say what you're working on, tag it, and go. A big, monospaced clock keeps the running time front and centre, wrapped in a focused dark theme with a single warm accent. No clutter, no dashboards you'll never open — just your time, tracked.

**Track in seconds**
• One tap to Start, Pause, Resume, and Stop
• A large, always‑readable running timer
• Add a short description to every session
• Build your own tag library and attach tags with a tap — remove them just as fast

**Live on your Lock Screen**
• A Live Activity shows your running session in the Dynamic Island and on the Lock Screen
• Glance at your elapsed time without ever opening the app

**Your data, your server**
• Connect Tagged to your own self‑hosted TimeTagger backend and your sessions sync straight to your timeline — ready for reporting on any TimeTagger client
• Records are saved when you stop, so a crash never leaves a ghost timer running on your server
• Test your connection right from Settings
• Privacy‑first: no account to create, no cookies, no third‑party tracking or analytics

**Made to feel right**
• Native iOS design with fluid, tactile controls
• Confirm‑before‑stop and keep‑screen‑awake options
• Manage, rename, and reorder your tags
• Full English, German, Italian, French, Portuguese, Spanish, Korean, Japanese and Chinese localization

Whether you're a freelancer, developer, student, or creator, Tagged uses flexible tags instead of rigid projects to keep tracking effortless — and keeps you in control of your own data.

_Tagged is a companion app for the open‑source TimeTagger project. A self‑hosted (or hosted) TimeTagger server is used for sync and reporting._
